Operation Retrieve

Welcome to the mission, AgentO.

Your task is to retrieve a crucial document that has been hidden in a suspect's home.

You will be guided by Nova, the liaison who will provide you with information and instructions throughout the mission.

Instructions

  1. Listen carefully to Nova's instructions and follow them closely.
  2. Use the direction buttons to move AgentO around the suspect's home.
  3. Search for clues that will help you find the document.
  4. Be stealthy and avoid being detected by the suspect or any security measures they may have in place.
  5. Once you have found the document, escape from the suspect's home undetected.
